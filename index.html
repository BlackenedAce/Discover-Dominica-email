Flying Here Is Easier Than Ever

Direct daily flights from Miami to Dominica are now available on American Airlines, connecting you from up to 40+ cities in North America. International flights from the US and Europe also connect through the Caribbean hubs of Antigua, Barbados, Martinique, St. Maarten, Puerto Rico, Guadeloupe and St. Lucia. Arrive and depart in comfort in our new executive lounge at Douglas Charles Airport

Travel Restrictions Eased For Visitors

We have removed all pre-arrival and arrival COVID-related requirements for vaccinated travelers. Simply present your COVID vaccine card and you’re free to roam. Unvaccinated visitors need to present negative results from an Antigen or PCR test. If the test is negative, no quarantine is needed so you can get out in nature right away.
